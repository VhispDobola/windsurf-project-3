## 13. Magma Sovereign (Lava Lord) - REDESIGNED
**Health:** 800 | **Color:** Brown/Orange
**Description:** A devastating lava-based entity with overwhelming volcanic attacks

### Phase 1 Attacks - Aggressive Foundation:
- **Magma Bombs:** Fast-moving projectiles that create lingering lava pools on impact (35 damage)
- **Lava Geysers:** Quick eruptions with minimal warning time (40 damage)
- **Molten Rain:** Area-wide falling lava drops (25 damage)
- **Heat Waves:** Expanding rings of fire from boss position (30 damage)

### Phase 2 Attacks - Pressure Tactics:
- **Tracking Magma:** Homing projectiles that accelerate over time (45 damage)
- **Lava Walls:** Moving barriers that shrink playable area (50 damage)
- **Volcanic Chain:** Eruptions that trigger nearby chain reactions (35 damage)
- **Superheated Ground:** Continuous damage zones that follow player movement (20 damage/tick)
- **Enhanced Heat Aura:** Stronger passive fire damage field

### Phase 3 Attacks - Ultimate Devastation:
- **Magma Vortex:** Pulls player toward center while dealing continuous damage (15 damage + pull)
- **Cascade Eruptions:** Screen-filling chain reaction of explosions (55 damage)
- **Lava Tsunami:** Massive wave crossing the entire screen (60 damage)
- **Final Inferno:** Ultimate combination attack triggering multiple attack types simultaneously (65+ damage)
- **Maximum Heat Aura:** Devastating passive fire damage field

### Attack Frequency Improvements:
- Phase 1: Attacks every 45-100 frames (much faster than original 120-180)
- Phase 2: Attacks every 60-120 frames with multiple simultaneous threats
- Phase 3: Attacks every 150-300 frames with ultimate devastating combinations

### Difficulty Enhancements:
- **Reduced Warning Times:** Minimal telegraph for quick attacks
- **Higher Damage Values:** 25-65 damage range vs original 15-40
- **Complex Mechanics:** Homing, pulling, area denial, and chain reactions
- **Visual Overload:** Multiple simultaneous effects create challenging scenarios
- **Strategic Positioning:** Attacks force player movement and positioning decisions

## General Attack Patterns:
- **Phase Transitions:** All bosses have 3 phases at 60% and 30% health
- **Telegraph Attacks:** Most dangerous attacks have warning indicators
- **Movement Patterns:** Bosses have unique movement styles
- **Damage Scaling:** Attack damage increases with phases
- **Visual Feedback:** Each attack has distinct visual effects

## Difficulty Notes:
- **Early Bosses (1-5):** Simpler patterns, more telegraphed attacks
- **Mid Bosses (6-10):** Complex mechanics, multiple attack types
- **Late Bosses (11-15):** Multi-stage, adaptive mechanics, ultimate attacks
